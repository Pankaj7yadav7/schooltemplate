Admission Overview

Thank you for expressing an interest in The My Public School . Investing in your children's education is one of the most important decisions you will make in your lifetime and one that requires great thought. These are formative years, and the relationships made, through friendships and interactions with teachers, will shape them for the future. It is critical that those sharing the responsibility for your children's education also share the values that are of primary importance in your home.At The "My Public School" l we offer a nurturing environment that fosters sharp learning skills, a top-of-the-line curriculum that offers the best in education.

The academic year for all classes commences in April and ends in March.

Admissions are open throughout the year, but are subject to availability. Your child can start school at any time during the academic year, except if seeking admission into CBSE Classes X and XII, As the academic year commences in April, maximum enrolments take place in the month Jan-Feb. We advise that you plan and complete the admission of your child in advance to avoid the rush period, as the Admissions Team reviews applications for new admissions from January each year. In order to take your interest for admission forward, we encourage you to make an appointment with our Admission Counsellors and visit our campuses. Our Admission Counsellors look forward to welcoming you to our campus for a tour and providing details about the school, curricular and extracurricular activities.



Feel free to visit our Counsellors during the following hours


Monday to Friday: 08am to 02.00 PM

Saturday: 08am to 12.00 PM

(Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays)

Alternatively you can also fill the enquiry form or the registration form online. Once the form is filled, please take a printout of the same, fill in the details and submit the hard copy of the completed form to the school.

Please note: Registration forms are also available in the school.



Admission Process

At My Public School , we have strived to make our admission process as simple as possible to ensure that all prospective parents enjoy a hassle free application experience.

Eligibility Criteria

Class with entry age at the start of academic session
*The age criteria for admission in different classes will be in accordance with NEP.

Step 1 : Admission Enquiry

In order to start with the admission process, we encourage you to make an appointment with our Admission Counsellors telephonically / walk-in / online and visit our campus. Our Admission Counsellors look forward in welcoming you to our campus for providing you with the details about the school, curricular and extracurricular activities and facilitate a tour about the campus.

  • To contact our Admission Counsellors telephonically, please call on 9826050161
  • Walk in enquiry, feel free to visit our Admission Cell during the following hours: Monday to Saturday: 10 am to 4 pm (Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays)

    • Step 2 : Registration

    The second step of admission process is registration of the child with the school for the year and class for which the admission is sought. The parents can purchase the registration form and school prospectus by paying the registration fee Rs 2100/- at the admission cell. Kindly carry two latest Passport size photographs of the child along with a copy of self-attested Date of Birth certificate. The registration fee is neither refundable nor adjustable and it is valid only for the specified academic year. Mere registration is not a guarantee of child’s admission.

    Step 3 : Registration Form Submission

    The parent needs to fill up the Registration Form and ensure that all the documents are in order. The following documents need to be submitted along with the Registration Form:

    1. Copy of the Birth Certificate of the Child
    2. Copy of Aadhar Card of the Child and the Parents
    3. Original TC from the previous school (applicable if the Admission gets confirmed)
    4. Copy of Report Card of previous class (applicable for children seeking admission from class I onwards)

    Step 4 : School Visit & Interaction/Entrance Exams

    Parents / Guardians are invited to visit the school campus to understand the culture and ethos of the Institution. A meeting can be scheduled with the class Coordinator / Hostel Superintendent (for boarding house admissions), so that the parents can have an interaction with the Coordinator / Hostel Superintendent, ask questions and collect any information they require.
    Each registered child should appear for the interaction/entrance tests which will be based on the academic syllabus of the previous class the child has passed.

    Step 5: Payment of Fees and Admission Confirmation

    Once the above steps are satisfactorily completed, parents can proceed with the final step of admission process i.e., pay the applicable fees for their ward to confirm admission.

    1. Students are admitted on First Come, First Served basis depending on availability of seats.
    2. Selected Students will be required to make the form submission and fee payment within 48 hours of the declaration of the interaction/entrance test results.
    3. The School fully reserves the right to Admissions and its decision in the matter is final and binding and cannot be questioned.
    Once after the completion of Fee submission and documentation, the admission is confirmed. The school will communicate with the parents via SMS/email to intimate about reopening of academic session, books and uniform details and about further formalities, to be completed, if any.

    Step 6: Parent Orientation Program

    There will be a parent orientation program before the beginning of the new academic session. This program will acquaint the parents with the Vision, Mission & Ethos of the school.
    All new entrants are required to produce a medical certificate certifying that they are free from any contagious or infectious disease and are otherwise physically fit to be admitted to a residential school. The parents must fill up Health Record provided by teacher and return it as soonest.
