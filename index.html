  • Education Programs

    Education programs are designed to facilitate learning and skill development in various subjects and disciplines. These programs can be formal, such as school or university curricula, or informal, like workshops, online courses, and training sessions. The goal is to impart knowledge, foster critical thinking, and promote academic or vocational growth.

  • Games Program

    Games programs focus on the development and creation of digital or non-digital games. This encompasses various aspects, including game design, programming, graphics, sound, and user experience. Games can be educational, entertaining, or a combination of both.

  • Labs Programs

    Labs programs are designed to provide hands-on, practical experiences in various scientific, technical, or research-oriented fields. These programs often involve experiments, research projects, and collaborative work in laboratory settings.
